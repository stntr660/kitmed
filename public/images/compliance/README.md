# Compliance Logos

This directory contains official certification and compliance logos for KITMED.

## Required Logos:

### ONSSA (Office National de Sécurité Sanitaire des produits Alimentaires)
- **File**: `onssa-logo.png`
- **Source**: https://www.onssa.gov.ma (official website)
- **Description**: Official Moroccan food safety authority logo
- **Size**: Recommended 48x48px minimum, preferably PNG format
- **Usage**: Authorization badge for medical equipment imports in Morocco

### ISO Certification Logos
Contact your certification body to obtain official logos for your certificates:

#### ISO 9001 - Quality Management System
- **File**: `iso-9001-logo.png`
- **Source**: Your ISO certification body (e.g., Bureau Veritas, SGS, etc.)
- **Size**: Recommended 48x48px minimum, preferably PNG format
- **Usage**: Quality management certification badge

#### ISO 13485 - Medical Devices Quality Management
- **File**: `iso-13485-logo.png`
- **Source**: Your medical device certification body
- **Size**: Recommended 48x48px minimum, preferably PNG format
- **Usage**: Medical devices quality certification badge
- **Note**: The component references ISO 12485 but uses ISO 13485 logo (standard practice)

#### ISO 22716 - Cosmetics Good Manufacturing Practices
- **File**: `iso-22716-logo.png`
- **Source**: Your cosmetics certification body
- **Size**: Recommended 48x48px minimum, preferably PNG format
- **Usage**: Cosmetics GMP certification badge

## Fallback System
The compliance badge component automatically falls back to gradient badges with text if logo files are not found, ensuring the page always displays correctly.

## Logo Guidelines
- Use official logos only if your organization holds valid certifications
- Respect logo usage guidelines from each certification body
- Maintain high quality (PNG format recommended)
- Ensure logos are readable at small sizes (48x48px)
- Keep aspect ratios intact when resizing
